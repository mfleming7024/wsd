How to make Chocolate Chip Cookies.

Ever wondered how to make chocolate chip cookies? Well if you follow our simple step by step instructions then you will be one step closer to having some chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix the butter and both sugars.
  3. Add the egg (beaten) and vanilla extract.
  4. Sift flour, baking soda and salt and add to the mixture.
  5. Mix thoroughly and add chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets and bake for 7-10 minutes.
