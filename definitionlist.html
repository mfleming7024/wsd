p
A paragraph tag. Contains copy text which can be used to tell something.
h1
The first heading tag used for the most important heading on the page.
h2
The second heading tag used for the second most important heading on the page.
h3
The third heading tag used for the third most important heading on the page.
li
Defines a list item to be used inside of a unordered list or an ordered list.
ol
The tag used for an ordered list in which the list items are arranged by numbers or other organized numerals.
ul
The tag used for an unordered list in which the list items aren't arranged by numbers or other organized numerals and instead they are organized with bullet points.
